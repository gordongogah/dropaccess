about us

great job

Drop Access is a women-led and youth-led NGO in kenya, founded in 2018 by two sisters who wanted to positively transform rural and grassroots communinities.Drop Access was legally incorporated by the NGO Coordination Board of Kenya in 2019

Our Mission

To promote energy access and climate action in the rural and off-grid communities through clean and climate-smart technologies

Our Vision

Resilient African communinities with 100% energy access and adaptive capacity to climate change

services

what we do

Improving agriculture

The Drop Access organization trains farmers on smart agricultural methods like irrigating dry lands, which helps them increase their production. The organization also prepares farmers with the necessary tools for successful extraction of information from sensora and prepare them for upcoming technology innovation in the sector

A portable solar fridge is an effective tool to improve food safety, prevent food waste and reduce post harvest loss. The Koyo Solar fridges are designed to bring down the cost of power, increase yeild and ensure food safety. Drop Access supplies Koyo solar fridges to small-scale farmers in developing countries to curb post harvest loss.

Improving the health care systems in rural communinities

With access to vaccines and lab test often unvailable at health centers in rural areas, the need for portable solar fridges is essential. Drop Access supplies vaccibox at an affordable price to marginalized health centers in Kenya.

To ensure affordable access to vaccines and treatment, we are teaming up with local microfinance institution(MFIs) to drive development of drop access supply programs. through this partnership, we will provide low interest loans to buy vaccibox equipments at low prices so you can help even more people have access to life saving vaccines

Providing access to energy to marginalized communinities

Provide training on how to build a solar powered systems using locally available materils and components

Provide technical support for installing solar power system in schools, clinics, community centers, churches, mosques and other building as wellas private houses.

Provide technical support for installing solar power systems on vehicles used in agriculture production and other uses such as cooking stoves that do not require electricity or fuel

vaccibox

vaccibox

Vaccobox, a portable solar fridge that can be mounted on a motorbike, bicycle or boat to safely store and transport vaccines, drugs and blood for transfussion to remote, hard to reache and off-grid rural communinities. The solution is also IoT-enable; intergrated with a communication and digital component that collects data in real time-time and store it on a cloud. This ensure that vaccines are kept in the best storage condition and conditions can also be traced, to promote effective healthcare programs in rural communinities
